## Execution History

Floogos keeps a detailed log of all executions of your flows.

## What does it include?

- Date and time of execution
- Result (successful or failed)
- Processed data (`data`)
- Node where a failure occurred (if applicable)
- Actions taken

## How to access

1. Go to the **History** section.
2. Select a flow to view its executions.
3. Click on a run to view the details

## Tips
- Use it for debugging
- Filter by errors to review recent bugs
